Big Mia

A Drinking Game for the Bold

What is Needed?

  • A pair of dice
  • A cup
  • Beverages of choices

Overview

Big Mia is played with a group of people (ideally 4-6 people) who take turns rolling a pair of dice under a cup and either honestly saying or lying what they rolled. Once a player has called a hand they are not allowed to touch the cup. The next player can either believe the called hand and accept the cup trying to roll a larger hand or call bullshit on the player lifting the cup to reveal the dice.

When do we drink?

Now we are getting to the important part. Every player has 3 lives to start, every time a play loses a life they drink their drink and the first person to lose all 3 lives has to take the "reward". The reward can be either a shotgunned beer or a shot put up by the group. When a player loses 3 lives the game starts over and everyone gets 3 lives. Big Mia is about survival!

Gameplay

The game starts with a player rolling the dice and peaking under the cup to see their hand. The player calls a hand and the next player clockwise can either accept the call or lift the cup. If the next player decides to roll, they must roll a higher or equal hand or lie about rolling a higher or equal hand. This continues until some eventually calls bullshit.
