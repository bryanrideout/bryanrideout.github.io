Big Mia

A Drinking Game for the Bold

What is Needed?

  • Pair of Dice
  • Cup
  • Beverages of Choice

Overview

Big Mia is played with a group of people (ideally 4-6 people) who take turns rolling a pair of dice under a cup and either honestly saying or lying what they rolled. Once a player has called a hand they are not allowed to touch the cup. The next player can either believe the called hand and accept the cup trying to roll a larger hand or call bullshit on the player lifting the cup to reveal the dice.

When do we drink?

Now we are getting to the important part. Every player has 3 lives to start, every time a play loses a life they drink their drink and the first person to lose all 3 lives has to take the "reward". The reward can be either a shotgunned beer or a shot put up by the group. When a player loses 3 lives the game starts over and everyone gets 3 lives. Big Mia is about survival!

Gameplay

Starting

The game starts with a player rolling the dice and peaking under the cup to see their hand. The player calls a hand and the next player clockwise can either accept the call or lift the cup. If the next player decides to roll, they must roll a higher or equal hand or lie about rolling a higher or equal hand. This continues until some eventually calls bullshit.

Losing a Life

A life is lost when player 2 calls out that player 1 is lying:

  1. If player 1 called what was under their cup then player 2 loses a life
  2. If player 1 lied about the hand under their cup then player 1 loses a life

Only the player after the person who has just rolled may call bullshit on their hand. The player next after the player that loses a life starts the next round.

The Random Roll

When you roll a hand lower than the previous player you have 2 options:

  1. Lie, saying you have a hand greater or equal to the previous hand
  2. Roll an additional random roll. When this is done you do not get to look or make a call on the dice. The next player now has a choice to pull the cup or roll to beat the hand you were originally trying to beat.

A player will usually pull if the hand you were trying to beat is high and roll against a low hand.

Calling a Big Mia

When a Big Mia is rolled now two lives are at stake. The next player can either:

  1. Call bullshit and the loser of this pull loses 2 lives
  2. Pass the cup to the next player and the round starts over but the player that passes loses 1 life

Say you have one life and the person before you calls a Big Mia, you cannot pass so if you believe them than you can try and roll to match their Big Mia. Remember you get 2 rolls your first roll and then random roll.

Dice Belong on the Table

If at anytime a player rolls the dice off the table the game is halted and that player must chug their drink. The game continues as normal after this is done.

The Finish

The first player to lose all 3 lives must take the reward (shotgun beer or a shot). The losing player then begins the next game.
